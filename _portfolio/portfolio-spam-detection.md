Developed a spam email detection system using NLP techniques including tokenization and vectorization (TF-IDF / Bag of Words) to transform raw email text into features. Trained and evaluated six supervised machine learning classifiers, comparing their accuracy and performance to identify the best model for real-world spam filtering.

## Tech Stack

`Python` `Jupyter Notebook` `Scikit-learn` `NLP` `TF-IDF` `Tokenization` `Supervised Learning`
